Sally Lawton is a moving image artist working in Detroit. Her worked has screened at The Nightingale Cinema, Echo Park Film Center, The Mini Microcinema, Onion City Film Festival, and others. She was a programmer for the Run of Life documentary series at Constellation Chicago and has done programming at other venues in Chicago and Detroit. She is currently a featured artist in the Detroit Neighborhood Arts Project.

She is also the co-director of Sincerely Productions. They create commercials, music videos, and alternatives to normal television. Visit sincerely.tv

Her first feature documentary was completed in January 2017, The Hard Earth, a examination of the revolution and current war in Ukraine.
